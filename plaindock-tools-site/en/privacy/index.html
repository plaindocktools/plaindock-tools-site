App ActiveMemo

1. Overview

ActiveMemo is a lightweight Windows desktop app for keeping your PC active during permitted work sessions and for taking quick local notes.

This Privacy Policy explains how ActiveMemo handles user data.

2. Data stored locally on your device

ActiveMemo stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Memo titles and memo contents
  • Memo order and update history
  • App settings, such as language and Keep Active settings
  • Import/export settings where applicable
  • Local free trial information, such as first launch date and last launch date
  • Subscription-related local status used to control app features
  • Error logs for troubleshooting

This data is stored on your Windows device, mainly under the local application data folder.

3. Memo contents

Your memo contents are stored locally on your device.

ActiveMemo does not upload your memo contents to our servers. ActiveMemo does not sell, share, analyze, or use your memo contents for advertising, user behavior analysis, or AI training.

4. Keep Active feature

When the Keep Active feature is enabled, ActiveMemo helps prevent sleep or display timeout during permitted work sessions.

This feature works locally on your device. It does not monitor your work activity, capture screen contents, record keystrokes, or send activity information to external servers.

Please use this feature in accordance with your organization’s rules and policies.

5. Subscription and payment

Subscriptions are processed through Microsoft Store.

Payment information, billing, renewal, cancellation, and payment method management are handled by Microsoft. ActiveMemo does not receive or store your credit card information.

The app may check your Microsoft Store subscription status to enable or restrict paid features.

6. Free trial information

ActiveMemo may store the first launch date and last launch date locally on your device to provide a limited free usage period.

This information is used only to determine whether the free usage period is active or expired.

7. Logs

ActiveMemo may create local log files for troubleshooting.

Logs are intended to contain technical error information. They are not intended to collect memo contents. If you contact support and choose to send log files, please review them before sending and avoid sharing sensitive information.

8. Support contact

If you contact us for support, we may receive information you choose to provide, such as your email address, message content, screenshots, app version, device information, or log files.

This information is used only to respond to your inquiry and improve the app.

9. Data sharing

We do not sell user data.

We do not share memo contents with third parties.

We may disclose information only when required by law or when necessary to protect the rights, safety, or security of users, the app, or the developer.

10. Children’s privacy

ActiveMemo is not specifically directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the effective date will be updated.
